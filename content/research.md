My research interests are rooted in mathematics, numerical analysis, and scientific computing. 
I currently develop and implement structure-preserving numerical methods, in particular compatible finite element discretizations, on complex domains.
My work aims to design stable and accurate algorithms that respect the underlying geometric and conservation properties of the continuous equations.
